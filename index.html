What we do

Everything you need to grow on short-form video.

⚡

Automatic Clip Discovery

We monitor top streaming content and identify the most-watched clips from the past 24 hours across categories and creators.

✂️

Smart Formatting

Clips are automatically reformatted to vertical 9:16, with auto-generated captions and platform-optimised titles using AI.

🚀

Multi-Platform Publishing

Processed clips are scheduled and published across YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels with platform-native metadata.

🤖

AI-Generated Metadata

Titles, descriptions, and hashtags are generated by AI models optimised for each platform's discovery algorithm.

📊

Performance Tracking

Every published clip is logged with views, platform, and status — giving you a clear picture of what's working.

🔒

Privacy First

We only access publicly available content. No user data is sold. All integrations use official OAuth 2.0 authorisation flows.

Process

From discovery to publication in minutes.

Discover

Top clips are fetched from public APIs — Twitch, YouTube and others — filtered by views and duration.

Process

Each clip is converted to vertical format, subtitled with Whisper AI, and given a branded title card.

Optimise

Platform-specific metadata is generated: hooks, hashtags, captions and descriptions tailored per network.

Publish

Clips are scheduled and posted via official APIs — TikTok Content Posting API, Meta Graph API, YouTube Data API.
